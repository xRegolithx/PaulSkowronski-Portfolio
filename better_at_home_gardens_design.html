Better At-Home Gardens Book Design

A cover developed for an e-book aimed toward young professionals who want to start their backyard garden.

I gave this cover a decidedly modern look, emphasizing “gardens” with a more organic typeface to subtly convey earthiness and nature, and sampled the earth-tone palette of the colors from the image to tie everything together, giving this cover a simple, yet warm and inviting look.
