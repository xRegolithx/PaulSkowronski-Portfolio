Bomb Balls Logo and Packaging

Bomb Balls are high-protein, keto-friendly snacks marketed toward a younger health-concious audience. The client wanted a fun, attention grabbing logo that would stand out from the current health-food trends of "clean and simple".

Playing off of the "Bomb Balls" brand, I gave a decidedly fun energy to the logo, as if the type is "shaking" from a high-energy explosion, and using a yellow/orange color scheme to really make this pop off of the shelf and grab people's attention.
