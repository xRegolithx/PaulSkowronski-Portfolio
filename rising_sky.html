Rising Sky Logo Design

Rising Sky is a digital currency trading platform startup that wanted to stand out from the rest.

Playing off of the phrase "to the moon", Rising Sky is meant to imply an upwards trajectory while staying within realistic confines when it comes to investing in digital currency.

The brand icon uses a purple to blue gradient to represent a future-leaning night sky, with dynamic white-space clouds and subtle shapes escaping it, to convey a sense of motion.
